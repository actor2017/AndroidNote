我用的3.0版本,3.1的没试过

1.把jar包加入libs目录

2.在MyApplication中初始化
        //初始化IMSDK
        IMClientManager.getInstance().initMobileIMSDK(Global.IP, Global.PORT_WS);

3.清单文件中

    <!--IMSDK-->
    <uses-permission android:name="android.permission.ACCESS_NETWORK_STATE"/>


        <!--IMSDK的Service-->
        <service
            android:name=".mobileimsdk.MobileImSdkService"
            android:enabled="true"
            android:label="MobileImSdkService">
            <intent-filter>
                <action android:name="com.kuchuan.wisdompolice.service.MobileImSdkService"/>
            </intent-filter>
        </service>

4.添加混淆:
##---------Begin: proguard configuration for MobileIMSDK---------
-keep class net.openmob.mobileimsdk.** {*;}
##---------End: proguard configuration for MobileIMSDK-----------

5.注意:
这个jar包用到了Gson,否则报错.
